Organic Farming



What is Organic Farming?

"Organic farming, also known as ecological farming or biological farming,[1][2][3][4][5] is an agricultural system that uses fertilizers of organic origin such as compost "

Organic Farming mainly rely upon:

  • Off farm wastes
  • Use of Crop residue
  • Animal manures
  • Legumes
  • Mineral bearing rocks
  • Green manures
  • Bio fertilizers

Advantages

  • Organic agriculture is a production system that sustains the health of soils
  • Organic farming relies more heavily on the natural breakdown of organic matter than the average conventional farm
  • Protecting the long term fertility of soils by maintaining organic matter levels, encouraging soil biological activity, and careful mechanical intervention
